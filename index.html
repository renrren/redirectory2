Technological Innovations in Safety

Introduction

Technology plays a critical role in enhancing safety in construction. From wearable devices to AI-powered analytics, innovations are transforming the industry.

Key Technologies

  • Wearable technology for monitoring worker health and safety.
  • AI and machine learning for predictive risk assessments.
  • Drone surveillance for real-time site monitoring.
  • Building Information Modeling (BIM) for hazard identification.
